STEP-BY-STEP GUIDE TO PREPARE DOSA

Step 1

Wash and soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 4-6 hours.

00:00

Step 2

Grind them into a smooth batter separately. Mix together and add salt. Ferment overnight.

00:00

Step 3

Heat a non-stick pan or griddle. Pour a ladleful of batter and spread it in a circular motion.

00:00

Step 4

Drizzle some oil around the edges. Cook until golden brown, then flip and cook the other side.

00:00

Step 5

Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.

00:00
